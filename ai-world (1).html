What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is the simulation of human intelligence in machines — enabling them to think, learn, and solve problems. From Siri to self-driving cars and ChatGPT, AI is already woven into daily life.

It encompasses Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision, and Robotics working together to build smarter systems.

⚠️ Disadvantages of AI

Challenges and risks we must address responsibly

01

Job Displacement

Automation threatens millions of jobs across manufacturing, transportation, and clerical work. Without reskilling programs, unemployment rates could rise sharply.

02

Privacy & Surveillance

AI-powered facial recognition and data harvesting raise serious privacy concerns. Governments and corporations can monitor citizens at unprecedented scale.

03

Bias & Discrimination

AI trained on biased data produces discriminatory results in hiring, lending, and criminal justice — reinforcing systemic inequalities at scale.

04

Lack of Emotional Intelligence

AI lacks true empathy, creativity, and moral judgment. In sensitive situations — mental health, ethical decisions — human nuance remains irreplaceable.

05

Security Threats & Deepfakes

AI can be weaponized to create convincing deepfakes, automate cyberattacks, and generate misinformation — posing major threats to democracy.

06

High Cost & Energy Use

Training large AI models requires enormous computing power, contributing to carbon emissions. The cost limits access to wealthy organizations only.

🔬 Types of AI

Understanding the different forms of artificial intelligence

🤖

Narrow AI

Designed for one specific task. Siri, Google Translate, spam filters. Most AI today falls here.

🧬

General AI

Hypothetical AI with human-level intelligence across all domains. Still a research goal.

🌌

Super AI

Theoretical AI that surpasses human intelligence in every way. Subject of intense global debate.

📡

Generative AI

Creates text, images, music, video from prompts. ChatGPT, DALL·E, Suno. Fastest-growing branch.
